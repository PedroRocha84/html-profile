My journey started in 1994, where i started to learn Windows 3.1 and MS-DOS. This was a moment where i noticed that i love to know: how things works; how things are made of.

  • In 2001 i built my first aplication, using Visual Basic language, for a company called Sovideo that was focused on setting windows permissions to increse system security. This software was deployed over +500 notebooks, in my country. Used at that time for Cyber Cafe's, reducing time spent on configurations, maintenance.
  • In 2011 i decided to try out IT Support and was my first job as a freelancer during 14 years, ending up at 2017.
  • In 2010 i finished my Master in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering in Porto University, never lost the eye of developing during this time, i did my first Chess Game using Visual Basic.
  • In 2010 i started my full time job as Product Engineer and also member of World Class Manufacturing and my continuous improvement skill arised.
  • Until 2014th i led a multi-disciplinary team of Engineers, where our focus were applying continuous improvement concepts to the current company process, an amazing journey
  • In 2017 a new chapter began in a Fashion Company where i was able to grow another skill, People and Team Management.

Most of you are at this stage thinking "Why to learn Web development?" it's for me a easy answer, we don't control our future, but we control what we can learn! For me learning something new, a side of my Family and Travel, is the most important thing for me that motivates me!
